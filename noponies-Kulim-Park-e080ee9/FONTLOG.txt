Basic Font Information

Kulim Park is a humanist sans serif typeface, with high x-height, open counter 'a', minimal degrees of contrast in stem width, inviting bowls and a design language aimed at encapsulating openness.

This typeface is the result of an exploration of how a local park redevelopment can inform a typographic design.

More detailed information can be found here: https://noponies.github.io/Kulim-Park/
